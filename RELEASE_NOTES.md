# ETR3 2025: Elytra Threat, Risk and Resilience Report

## Overview
ETR3 2025 presents a clear, decision-focused view of the cybersecurity threat landscape and resilience priorities not just for India, with global context. It is designed for boards, CXOs, CISOs, and operational leaders who need facts, clarity, and action.

## Highlights
- Material threat vectors and attack pathways for 2025
- Global exposure themes with sector notes
- Control priorities aligned to ISO 27001 and CERT-In expectations
- Practical resilience checklist for leadership and SOC teams
- Concise figures and tables for briefings and playbooks
